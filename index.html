01 · about

About Me

Bridging backend architecture with seamless frontends.

I'm a 2026 B.E. Computer Science & Engineering graduate from Hindusthan Institute of Technology, working as a Full-Stack Developer who specializes in secure, scalable web applications. My focus sits at the intersection of three things: backend architecture, AI-assisted document processing, and secure data transmission — engineering that has to work quietly and correctly, every time.

My path has run from architecting REST APIs in Django, Flask and Node.js, to building the React interfaces that sit on top of them, to digging into the cybersecurity protocols — AES-256 encryption chief among them — that keep the data in between safe. Recent work has included shipping a live, production restaurant ordering platform for a real client, and building an OCR pipeline that turns handwritten notes into structured digital text.

"Good code, like a good chess game, is decided by the moves you make before the crisis." — my own rule of thumb, most days

Away from the keyboard, I'm a competitive chess player and coach, training the next generation of players — a habit that shows up in how I approach engineering problems too: think several moves ahead, and don't move a piece you haven't fully considered.
